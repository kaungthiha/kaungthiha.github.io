What I'm Up To

Nowadays, I like playing around with data to build cool products. I've had a bunch of experiences close to product (prod ops + data products) but never a formal PM position — though I'm trying to change that. In the meantime, I take a product-minded approach in my day-to-day work, plus the small utilities I'm cooking alongside AI dev tools. That ranges from drafting scoping docs and PRDs to socializing analyses across my C1 org. Across every job, the awesome product people I've met all connect dots across multiple facets of a business — which is why I ask a lot of questions to build context.

I've also got a solid grasp of Python and Java, and I'm pretty handy with SQL, R, and Tableau. Lately I'm digging into the data engineering side (shoutout to Zach Wilson, who is putting out some awesome stuff). Speaking in both technical and non-technical terms is my biggest strength — and one I work on every day.
