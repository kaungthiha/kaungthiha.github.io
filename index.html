What I'm Up To

Nowadays, I like playing around with data to build cool products. I've had a bunch of experiences close to product(prod.ops + data products) but never a formal PM position though I am trying to change that. In the meantime, I try to take a product minded approach in my day to day work, as well as the small utilities I'm cooking alongside AI dev tools. This ranges from helping draft scoping documents or taking a stab at PRDs to socializing my analyses to multiple stakeholders across my C1 org. I've been realizing that across all my work experiences, awesome product people are able to connect the dots across multiple facets of a business which is why I try to ask a lot of questions to build context.

I've also got a solid understanding of Python and Java + am pretty handy with SQL, R and Tableau. Been trying to understand the data engineering side of things a bit more as well (Shoutout to Zach Wilson who is putting out some awesome stuff!!) I think being able to speak in both technical and nontechnical terms is my biggest strength but also one that I work on everyday.
